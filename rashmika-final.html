The Beginning · UMSL 2021

I'm Rashmika — a Computer Science graduate from the University of Missouri–St. Louis, graduating May 2025, with a Minor in Mathematics and an AI Certification. I arrived in the US as an international student with one clear mission: use data to solve problems that actually matter.

The Curiosity · 2022–2023

Not mental health statistics in a paper — but real classifiers that predict depression from a student's lifestyle. Not fraud theory — but unsupervised algorithms that catch it with zero labels. Not pre-built sklearn pipelines — but neural networks built from closed-form equations, derived by hand, line by line, until the math made sense.

The Builder · 2024–Present

Today I've shipped 8+ ML projects, achieving 89.47% accuracy on medical classification, 94.25% F1-score on depression prediction, and built Tableau dashboards that turn 8,807 rows of Netflix data into self-service executive intelligence. I don't wait to be taught. I build to learn.
